WELCOME TO Pravasi Jharkhand

Connecting Roots. Celebrating Identity. Welcome to Pravasi Jharkhand, a dedicated platform for Jharkhandis living across the globe. Whether you are miles away or just a state apart, this is your digital home— bringing together the vibrant culture, traditions, and aspirations of our people. Here, we celebrate our roots, share success stories of the diaspora, promote opportunities for collaboration, and build a strong network of Jharkhandis worldwide. Be part of cultural exchange programs, social initiatives, and professional engagements that aim to uplift our community.

Dr. Ravindra Kumar Ray

Founder

Dr. Ravindra Kumar Ray is a seasoned statesman, visionary thinker, and the proud founder of Pravasi Jharkhand. With decades of experience in public service and grassroots leadership, he has tirelessly worked to uplift the people of Jharkhand—both within the state and across the globe. Currently serving as the President of BJP Jharkhand, Dr. Ray has also represented Koderma as a Member of Parliament and held key portfolios as a Minister in the Jharkhand Government.
